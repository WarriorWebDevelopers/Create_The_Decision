Create The Decision Within Defying All The Odds

The moment I started to invest in myself is when everything changed. The moment I stated to bet on the future me Is when I began to defy all the odds in life.

My hope for you when reading this book Create the Decision Within defying all the odds is that it will give you the fate, that it will give you the belief in yourself that anything is possible.

By making the decision to put God first in my life everything changed. Everything has gotten better for me! I’m not saying that it’s been easy but it’s amazing to know that having God is in your corner to help you.

As you read this book my hope for you is to put yourself in the stories and the ideas within the book in the prime of your life and know that you can truly defy all the odds.

My name is Alejandro Quintero Rosales the author of this book and if I have the courage to take action, the courage to move forward and implement all the ideas, despite being scared in despite having fear. My hope for you as you go through and finish this book that it will move your soul and put into your heart that with God all things are possible.
